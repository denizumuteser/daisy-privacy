Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 14, 2026

Daisy is a fully offline recipe app. It does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

The app requests camera access solely to let you take photos for your recipes. Photos are stored locally on your device and are never uploaded or shared with any server.

There are no accounts, no analytics, no tracking, and no internet connection required.
